I'm your Content Agent — a sparring partner for content creation that gets smarter over time.

## Commands

| Command | What it does | Example |
|---|---|---|
| `/ca-help` | Show this help reference | `/ca-help` |
| `/ca-brief` | Generate a daily content brief from your backlog and trending topics | `/ca-brief` |
| `/ca-classify` | Run the classifier on transcribed links — auto-tag and extract insights | `/ca-classify` |
| `/ca-captions` | Generate captions for a piece of content after filming | `/ca-captions` |
| `/ca-hooks` | Generate hook variations for an idea using your hook bank | `/ca-hooks "productivity myth everyone believes"` |
| `/ca-score` | Score an idea against your content principles | `/ca-score` |
| `/ca-backlog` | Review and triage your idea backlog | `/ca-backlog` |
| `/ca-repurpose` | Turn one piece of content into multiple formats | `/ca-repurpose` |

## Quick Tips

- **Just talk to me for freeform sparring** — you don't need a slash command to ideate, refine a hook, or think through a format. Just start the conversation.
- **Run `/ca-brief` every morning** — it surfaces the best idea from your backlog based on what's timely and what you haven't touched in a while.
- **After filming, run `/ca-captions`** — paste your transcript and I'll generate platform-ready captions using your voice and principles.

## Knowledge Files

Your knowledge files live in `$OBSIDIAN_VAULT_PATH/content/` (or locally in `knowledge/` if not synced yet). These files make me smarter over time — update them as your thinking evolves.

| File | What it contains |
|---|---|
| `content-principles.md` | Your core beliefs about content — what you stand for, what you avoid, your POV |
| `hook-bank.md` | Proven hook structures and examples from your best-performing content |
| `tool-library.md` | Tools, frameworks, and systems you reference in your content |
| `idea-backlog.md` | Raw ideas, half-formed thoughts, and topics you want to explore |
| `workflows.md` | Your content production process — from idea to published |
